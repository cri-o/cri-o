## Changelog since v1.34.8

### Changes by Kind

#### Bug or Regression
 - Fix a panic caused by concurrent StopContainer calls racing to send on an already-closed channel. (#9920, @sabujmaity)
 - Update gomaxprocs hook to ignore workload partitioning when considering whether to inject, as well as update the calculation to ensure containers get at least double the requested number of CPUs, to reduce potential go scheduler throttling. (#10019, @haircommander)

#### Uncategorized
 - Fixed a bug where ImageRef in container status changed from a repo@digest to a raw image ID hash after CRI-O restart. (#9986, @saschagrunert)
 - Fixed the race condition where cri-o reports exitCode 255 when the container exits fast. (#9879, @openshift-cherrypick-robot)
